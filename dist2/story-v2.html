Our Story

A Decade of Us

2016 Year One

Two young college students decided, after just one month, that this was something worth choosing. It was the beginning of us.

We look so young!

2018 Year Three

Our dates were wonderfully ordinary: long walks through the city, quiet hours in the library, and crossing Beijing from one end to the other just to spend a little more time together.

2020 Year Five

A pandemic and long distance put our love to the test. Between closed borders, face masks, and the uncertainty of it all, we learned how to fill our small apartment with joy, laughter, and love — even if it meant cutting each other's hair.

Excuse our bad hair!

2022 Year Seven

Our little family grew by four paws. Haku came into our lives and made everything a little louder, a little messier, and a lot sweeter.

Haku's first road trip

2024 Year Nine

This year brought its share of challenges. When Frank went through surgery and a difficult season of recovery, Patrick was there every step of the way. More than ever, it was a time that showed us what "in sickness and in health" truly means.

2017 Year Two

Sri Lanka, the pearl of the Indian Ocean, was where we took our first trip together. In the warmth of the tropics, with the sea all around us, our love grew deeper and warmer.

Sinhagiri, the Lion Rock

2019 Year Four

So much changed this year. We graduated, traveled to South Korea, began documenting our life together, and set off for different countries to continue our studies. Life began pulling us in new directions, but somehow, we kept finding our way back to each other.

2021 Year Six

At last, we were reunited. We closed the distance, bought a home of our own, and began building the life we had long imagined together.

I'll Be There
For You

2023 Year Eight

This was a year of beauty and wonder, of collecting moments we knew would stay with us forever: sunrises and sunsets by the sea, a rainbow cast across Niagara Falls, the Gemini meteor shower overhead, countless lanterns rising into the sky, the fall colors in the Smoky Mountains, and Christmas lights in New York City.

First time camping,!

2025 Year Ten

In February, beneath the Northern Lights in Fairbanks, Frank proposed to Patrick, with the stars above bearing witness to our promise.

surprised!

2026 Now

And now, after ten years, in October, we invite the people we love most, who have held us and carried us here, to witness as we step into the rest of our story.
